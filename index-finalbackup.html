Work + Life Overview

I feel naturally connected to many places all at once, as I have happily developed first-hand familiarity with quite a few major cities. Through stimulating, startling bursts of experiences, I grew up, studied and worked, very frequently on-the-move, from one gripping place to another: notably Johannesburg, London, Paris, New York, London again, Berlin, with some vivid, amusing, or absurd pitstops on-the-way.

The major highlight of my career was my co-creation and then flying-solo management of my own London Video Prod Startup, to which I was whole-heartedly committed day-in-day-out, and which represented a sensational opportunity for me, with my great team, to reliably produce all kinds of impactful, professional, and purposeful Audio-Visual content.

As the Technical Director, and sometimes Stage Manager, of multiple Festivals, I took much pride in ensuring that all simultaneous events would have pristine customized setups, arranged by me and my on-hand ultra-dependable team.

I have had the good fortune of compiling a variety of stimulating, challenging, fulfilling work-experiences.
