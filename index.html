Q and A

Background

Beyond my core skillset, what do I add to the team-mix?

Through self-testing, lucky, ridiculous, stimulating choices, lots of city-hopping, basically through my mish-mash of experiences, I now have my own unique approach to all kinds of interactions. Very open-minded, and with some healthy self-belief, I love taking on challenges with people from everywhere.
Having vibrantly bounced from city-to-city, in all that 'rumble-tumble', around people with a medley of roots, like me, I naturally developed good instincts around them, whatever their sensibilities, culture, humor, beyond my pure work know-how.

What's my take on project-collaborations?

As much as I relish taking the initiative out of all-out commitment, I also feel very fulfilled co-creating something with stimulating collaborators, as the buzz from that kind of work-relationship can be incredibly engrossing and inspirational.
In my mind, when everyone is more or less on the same wavelength, humming along in some kind of harmonious craze, that type of team-blending can feel very rewarding, like really living-and-breathing work – and getting a kick out of it.
So, in short, I'm potentially very motivated and stimulated, with everyone thrashing the right chords, as we charge with urgency and humour towards the finish-line.

Broadly-speaking, which projects appeal to me?

I love all challenges, especially the 'visual' kind, also what highlights politics past or present, and whatever radiates to some kind of all-out creative beat, in other words, nearly 'anything goes', the fresher and more multi-faceted the better.
Each project is above all else a great opportunity for me to whole-heartedly challenge myself.
As my track-record highlights, the purpose of my work has varied immensely, from the revealing, in-depth, classical kind, to the fun, experimental, edgy sort, from vibrant and gritty sites, to impactful promos, documentaries, and musical montages, all my projects have been full of insights for me, building up my profile-mix.

My 'in-the-thick-of-things' engagement?

From freeing team-strategizing, to vibrant creative workflows, my core purpose is always to make the project-experience highly productive, smooth, and fresh.
It is to come up with a clear 'big picture' plan-of-action, while engaging whole-heartedly, lock, stock, and barrel with the project-at-hand, making the most it – and getting a rush out of my ‘in-the-thick-of-things’ engagement.
I love feeling the collaborative team-spirit in the heat of the deadline-conquering-action, the motivating focus, professional camaraderie, in an atmosphere which is upbeat, encouraging and challenging, for people who give it their all.
Ultimately, I dive off the springboard with my passionate ‘hands-on’ attitude, to make others feel that I am all-in, with positive, down-to-earth zeal, and absurd-takes-loving 'good cheer'.
